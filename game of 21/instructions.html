Game of 21

Instructions

Welcome to the Game of 21. Here's how to play:

  1. User starts by entering a number from 1 to 3. They can enter up to three numbers at a time.
  2. Next user enters a number 1 greater than the last number entered by the previous user.
  3. This continues until a user reaches 21.
  4. The user who reaches 21 loses the game.
